Creamy Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Add eggs and cream to a small mixing bowl. Season to taste. Whisk briskly.
  2. Heat a small saut√© pan over medium heat.
  3. Add butter to the pan. Swirl until it's melted, but don't let it brown.
  4. Add egg mixture to the pan. Turn gently. Cook to desired consistency. Don't over-mix. This will cause the eggs to stick to the pan.
  5. Scoop cooked eggs on a plate alongside warm buttered toast.
